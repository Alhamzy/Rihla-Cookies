The Heart of Muscat.

The Majlis Gold

Our origin story. This cookie captures the hospitality of the Omani Majlis. We use premium saffron threads and hand-ground cardamom, folded into a dough that is soft as the dunes of the Sharqiya Sands. Topped with a single leaf of 24k gold, it is our tribute to the art of the welcome.
